Milkshake

Description

A milkshake is a creamy, chilled beverage made by blending milk with ice cream and flavourings such as chocolate, vanilla, or fruit. It has a smooth, thick texture and is often topped with whipped cream, syrups, or sprinkles to enhance its taste and appearance. Milkshakes are popular as both a refreshing drink and a sweet dessert, enjoyed by people of all ages.

This versatile drink can be customised in many ways depending on personal preference. Common variations include strawberry, banana, chocolate, and cookies-and-cream milkshakes. Served cold in a tall glass with a straw, milkshakes are perfect for hot days or as a treat alongside meals or snacks.

  • Milk
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Vanilla extract (or vanilla syrup)
  • Sugar (optional, depending on sweetness)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Sprinkles and Cherries
  • Ice cubes (optional, for extra thickness)
